PokéVault Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

PokéVault ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Pokémon TCG collection tracker developed by Luxiven. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information — email address and display name, used to authenticate you via Supabase.
  • Collection data — card IDs, quantities, conditions, purchase prices, and notes you enter. Stored in our Supabase database and locally on your device.
  • Expense data — pack purchase records (set name, quantity, price, date) you log manually.
  • Camera frames — processed locally on-device via ML Kit OCR to read card collector numbers. No camera frames are transmitted to any server.
  • Price lookups — card IDs are sent to pokemontcg.io to fetch market prices. No personal data is included in these requests.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Contacts or other apps on your device
  • Any data from your camera beyond the collector number read during a scan

3. How Data Is Stored

Your account and collection data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security enabled — only you can read or modify your own data. A local SQLite cache on your device stores card information to reduce API calls; this cache contains no personal data.

Authentication tokens are stored in Android Keystore via Expo SecureStore and are never written to AsyncStorage or shared storage.

4. Third-Party Services

  • pokemontcg.io — provides card data and market prices. Requests include card IDs and search queries only. See their privacy policy.
  • eBay Browse API (optional) — used to show recent sold prices for cards on your wishlist. Requests include card names only. See eBay's privacy policy.
  • Supabase — our backend provider. Data is hosted in the EU (Frankfurt). See Supabase's privacy policy.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile → Settings screen. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

6. Children

PokéVault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us at the address below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
