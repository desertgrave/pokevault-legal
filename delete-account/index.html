Delete Your PokéVault Account

Last updated: June 2026

You can delete your PokéVault account and all associated data directly from within the app at any time. No email or web form is required.

⚠️ Deletion is permanent. Once confirmed, your account, collection, wishlist, decks, trades, grading submissions, and expense records are immediately and irreversibly deleted.

How to delete your account

1
Open PokéVault Launch the app on your Android device and sign in if prompted.
2
Go to Profile Tap the Profile icon in the bottom navigation bar (bottom right).
3
Tap "Delete Account" Scroll to the bottom of the Profile screen and tap Delete Account.
4
Confirm deletion A confirmation dialog will appear. Tap Delete to permanently delete your account and all data.

What data is deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your entire card collection (all entries, conditions, prices, notes)
  • Your wishlist and target prices
  • Your decks and deck card lists
  • Your trade log
  • Your grading submissions
  • Your pack purchase and expense records
  • Your profile (display name, currency preference)

What is not deleted

  • Anonymised, aggregated price data (no personal identifiers)
  • Locally cached card images on your device — clear your app data via Android Settings to remove these

Need help?

If you are unable to access the app to delete your account, contact us at support@luxiven.io with the email address associated with your account and we will delete it manually within 30 days.
